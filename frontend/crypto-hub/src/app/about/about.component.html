About Crypto Hub

Crypto Hub is a modern single-page application (SPA), offering users an intuitive interface to explore and manage cryptocurrency data. Whether you're a casual user or an expert, Crypto Hub helps you stay informed and organized.

Explore Cryptocurrencies

Search for specific cryptocurrencies and view detailed information in real-time.

Manage Records

Easily create, edit, and track your crypto-related entries within the app.

Secure Role-Based Access

With a role-based authentication system, only admins can manage cryptocurrencies and approve blog articles.
